# Daily Musts — the non-negotiables

> These reset every day. Each day's log (`logs/YYYY-MM-DD.md`) copies this checklist and tracks what's done. The check-in always surfaces whatever is still open.

## Every single day
- [ ] **Empty the dishwasher** when it's clean (check morning / midday / pre-dinner). _Don't let her do it._
- [ ] **Take out the garbage** (easiest while walking the dog).
- [ ] **Tidy as I go** — leave no mess; put my things away; clear surfaces I used.
- [ ] **One caring act for my wife** (see `context/relationship-goals.md` → daily menu).
- [ ] **One proactive household task** beyond the above (see `context/household.md` → wins).
- [ ] **Ask my wife about her day** and actually listen.

## Pet care
- [ ] Dog walked (~11:00 — preferred time).

## Why these three are the headline
Dishwasher, garbage, tidiness — these are the small recurring things that, when I drop them, create friction and land on her. **Nailing these every day is the fastest way to reduce conflict and show I've got the home handled.**
