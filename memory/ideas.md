# Ideas Backlog

> A bank of ready-to-use ideas so I'm never stuck for "what could I do for her?" When I run `/idea` or ask for ideas, pull from here (and add new ones). Mark used ones with the date so check-ins can rotate fresh ones.

## Quick wins (≤5 min, any day)
- Genuine specific compliment about something she did today.
- Make/bring her tea or her favorite drink.
- Hug from behind while she's cooking/standing.
- Take a chore off her plate silently.
- Warm text mid-workday: "thinking about you and the bump 💛".
- Tidy a room before she notices it needed it.

## Medium (an evening / small spend)
- Cook or take charge of dinner + full cleanup.
- Run her a bath; foot or back rub after.
- Bring home a small treat or flowers. (She loves **meloa** — easy win.)
- Plan a cozy movie/series night with her favorite snacks.
- Handle an errand she's been meaning to do.
- Wash her car for her — she'll notice and appreciate it. — added 2026-07-02

## Bigger (planned)
- A **babymoon** or weekend away before the baby comes.
- A proper date night (pregnancy-friendly restaurant / experience).
- A thoughtful gift tied to something she mentioned wanting.
- Set up something for the nursery as a surprise.

## Pregnancy-specific
- Take over the physically hard chores without being asked.
- Pillow/comfort upgrades for sleep.
- Go to an appointment fully present and engaged.
- A small gift for *her* (not just the baby) to mark a milestone week.
