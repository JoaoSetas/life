# Idea

Help João care for his wife thoughtfully.

## If he's asking for ideas
1. Read `memory/ideas.md`, `context/relationship-goals.md`, and `context/pregnancy.md` (so suggestions fit how far along she is).
2. Offer **2–3 specific, doable** ideas matched to the moment (time available, day of week, budget hinted). Prefer ideas **not** in the "Used recently" list. Make them concrete ("run her a bath and rub her feet" — not "be nice").
3. If he picks one, log it: add it to today's log and to `memory/ideas.md` under "Used recently" with today's date. Optionally add a reminder via the same logic as `/remember`.

## If he's giving you an idea to save
- Add it to the right section of `memory/ideas.md` and confirm.

Keep it warm and real. Small consistent gestures beat grand rare ones — say so when relevant.
