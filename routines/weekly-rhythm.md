# Weekly Rhythm

> The shape of a typical week, so check-ins can adapt to the day.

## Monday — Porto (in person)
- Travel to Porto, work **in person**. Longer/harder day, away from home.
- **Extra effort needed at home-by-proxy:** send my wife a warm message during the day; she's solo with the house, pets, and pregnancy.
- Before leaving: empty the dishwasher, take the garbage on the way out, leave things tidy.
- Coming home: arrive present (not still in work mode). Greet her properly. Ask about her day first.

## Tuesday–Friday — remote / usual (_confirm_)
- ~8h work. Use natural breaks (coffee, lunch) for quick household checks (dishwasher!) and a warm text.
- Transition out of work deliberately at the end of the day — close the laptop, change context, be home in mind not just body.

## Weekend
- Bigger proactive contributions: deeper cleaning, errands, batch chores so the week is lighter for her.
- Quality time: a small date or cozy plan. Pregnancy-friendly outing.
- Plan/prep for the week ahead (groceries, pet supplies, appointments).

## Weekly intentions (review each Sunday)
- Did she feel cared for this week? What's one thing to do better next week?
- Any appointments, errands, or prep coming up? (check `context/pregnancy.md`)
- Log the week's wins in `memory/wins.md`.
