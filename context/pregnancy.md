## How to support her right now (2nd trimester)
- **Physical:** take over heavy/awkward/bending tasks, litter boxes (cat litter is a real pregnancy no-go), carrying, anything strenuous. Offer back/foot rubs.
- **Rest:** protect her sleep and downtime; take initiative on chores so she can put her feet up.
- **Emotional:** check in on how she *and* the baby feel. Be patient — mood/energy swings are physiological.
- **Engagement:** be present and interested at appointments and scans; talk about the baby, names, plans.
- **Nutrition/comfort:** keep her favorite (pregnancy-safe) snacks stocked; stay hydrated together.

## Looking ahead (so I'm proactive, not caught off guard)
- Prenatal appointments & scans — know the dates, show up engaged.
- Nursery prep, baby gear, hospital bag.
- A **babymoon** / special time together before the baby arrives.
- Birth plan conversations.
